How to use mailbox.AI

3 simple steps:

  • Scan your incoming mail, e.g. with your iPhone
  • Upload your files and let mailbox.AI do OCR and content recognition
  • Use simple rules to have mailbox.AI remember your choices

  • Hotkeys
  • Tipps




    • Did you know that your iPhone has a scan function?

    Use it to save scans of your documents to the "download" folder. You can use the iCloud "download" folder to have the files available on your Mac immediately.

    Upload your files

    Start mailbox.AI ony your Mac and upload your files from iCloud. The app will do an automatic OCR on each file and investigate its contents.

    Select the contents to be remembered

    For each tag and level, you can assign one or more indicators that will automatically create the right tags the next time you upload a similar document.

    Browse your files

    mailbox.AI will remember the contents and tags for your files. Use tags to track activites, follow up on documents etc.

    Friendly format, no dependencies

    All files are stored as PDFs in a classic folder structure, along with a simple .txt file with all the tags and text contents.
